Haluski

This simple Polish recipe is a classic and cheap comfort food.

Ingredients
1 yellow onion, diced
1 stick salted butter, separated into two halves
1/2 bag wide egg noodles 
1/2 head green cabbage, roughly chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Preparation
1. Boil 4 quarts water in a large pot. Add egg noodles when boiling and boil until noodles are tender.
2. Melt 1/2 of the butter in a saute pan on low-medium heat. Add onion when butter is sizzling. Saute until browned.
3. Remove onion and set aside. Add chopped cabbage and remaining butter and saute until browned. 
4. Drain noodles in a colander and rinse to remove starch. 
5. In a large bowl, toss noodles, onions, and cabbage until mixed. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
